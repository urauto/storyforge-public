## About the Game

Storyforge is an interactive, choice-based adventure game where your decisions shape the story.

Explore immersive narratives across fantasy realms, deep oceans, distant galaxies, haunted locations, and survival scenarios. Every choice you make influences how the story unfolds, leading to unique outcomes and replayable experiences.

Start new adventures, continue ongoing campaigns, and discover how different decisions create entirely new story paths.
